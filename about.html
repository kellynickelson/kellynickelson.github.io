About Me

Hello!

I'm Kelly Nickelson, a Senior Business Intelligence Engineer driven by a passion for harnessing data to fuel organizational success. I graduated from Kalamazoo College in June 2021 with a degree in Biology and concentrations in Environmental Studies and Community & Global Health. K College provided me with a rigorous and interdisciplinary education, extending my knowledge beyond the traditional classroom through experiences like studying abroad in Thailand and interning at the Chaing Mai Breathe Council.

Post-graduation, I joined Michigan State University's Entomology lab as a Research assistant, delving deeper into my passion for research, particularly in working with data. This led me to Tempus AI, a company dedicated to improving cancer patient outcomes through AI.

I began my career at Tempus AI as an Operations Data Analyst, where I quickly learned to translate my scientific rigor into business impact. This role solidified my belief in the transformative power of data-driven strategy and led me to transition into the Business Intelligence team. As an engineer, I now combine my biology background with a high-level technical stack to architect the data models that power our laboratory and analytical reporting.

Working in the biotech startup environment has helped me hone my skills in thriving within fast-moving and dynamic settings. My mindset is geared towards innovation, agility, and a proactive approach to driving positive change.

Outside of the office, I’m usually rock climbing, reading, or on the volleyball court. I’m a loyal Detroit sports fan and a dedicated Chicago Sky supporter - which makes for a fun emotional rollercoaster most seasons. I spend my weekends exploring the city’s newest restaurants and am always open to recommendations!

Feel free to explore my recent projects, and thank you for your time.
